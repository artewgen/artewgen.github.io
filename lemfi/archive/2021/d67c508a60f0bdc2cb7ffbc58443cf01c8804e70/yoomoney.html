YooMoney (ex.Yandex.Money) 2019 – Present, Saint Petersburg
Design processes of payment solutions in YooKassa and B2C utility bills project

YooMoney (ex Yandex.Money) company is a Russian electronic payment service that helps users pay for goods and services, and companies accept online payments. YooMoney is the most popular service in Runet in accepting payments on the Internet, based on the number of sites, MARC data for 2019.

Scope of work

During my work in B2B, I redesigned projects such as the Embedded checkout form (Widget), Redirect form and Mobile SDK. Also, I designed YooKassa payment management app.

Responsibilities

Checkout Widget Redesign

YooMoney widget is the company's latest payment acceptance solution. It's a ready-made form for websites that provides seamless payments without switching to other tabs. The widget is used by Adidas & Reebok, iHerb, Cian, Renault and other big names.

Problems and solutions

The previous version of the widget is outdated, did not suit users and businesses, it was decided to make a redesign. After conducting the user interviews and tests, collecting their wishes and problems, we identified major goals for the redesign: optimization, improvement and extension.

Redesign results

  • major product and UX issues are closed;
  • conversion rate increased more than 5%;
  • new features: loyalty, scalability, re-election etc.

Additional goal — the concept of the widget was re-used in more than 8 company projects.

You can find a full case of the widget redesign on Behance .

YooKassa Mobile App

YooKassa app is a payment management center for merchants. It's an easy way to manage payments, issue invoices, make refunds, monitor payment and invoice analytics.

Problems and solutions

Users of the YooKassa did not have the opportunity to monitor the statistics of their sites on mobile devices, because the site with the personal account of YooKassa does not have an adaptive version. Many requests were received from merchants for the mobile version and it was decided to make a mobile application on iOS and Android.

During interviews with users, the main features for the application were identified:

  • full control over payments;
  • analytics for payments and invoices;
  • fast invoicing;
  • refunds;
  • feedback and support.
