Medical Service App Design

Mobile and web application for the patients and doctors that allows making online consultations and purchase. Design was based on full UX research, included generation of ideas, work with a buyers, prototyping, interactions and visual design.

Scope of work

  • highlighting customer's goals (SMART methodology) and user's goals;
  • outlining user's roles and drawing up map of influence;
  • segmenting target audience;
  • studying competitors;
  • building an application information schema;
  • creating more than 40 screen prototypes and making visual design;
  • designing presentation on Behance with animation.
